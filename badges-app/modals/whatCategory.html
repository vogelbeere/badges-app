What is a category?

Categories align to the Brookes Guiding Principles: Confidence, Enterprising Creativity, Connectedness and Generosity of Spirit. Each activity that you complete earns you an achievement that has been assigned to one of these principles.

Achievements you have earned are displayed in bold colour with a star; activities you have signed up for, but have not yet completed, are displayed as faded with a tick.

Select the category headings to see a list of activities in that category.
